## 💡 Pro Tips

**For Interview Prep:**
- Read sample answers out loud 3 times
- Record yourself answering and review
- Practice with a mock interviewer
- Memorize key points, not full answers
- Focus on your own examples

**For Learning Roadmap:**
- Start with Phase 1 fundamentals
- Build the SaaS project alongside learning
- Commit to the daily structure
- Deploy early and often (even if just to Heroku)
- Document your learning journey on GitHub
